About Us

In the enchanting realm of interior architecture, where artistry meets innovation, we are inspired by the fusion of numerical poetry and the canvas of creative expression, our name embodies the spirit of artistic exploration within the world of design.


We see ourselves as the life-giving veins that course through our bodies, which signifies the pulsating energy that breathes life into spaces. It represents the intertwining of art and interior architecture, where each stroke of inspiration becomes an integral part of the design tapestry.


8978 is not any lottery combination, but a numerical bonding that is carefully woven into our identity, unveiling a captivating symphony of symbolism. Each digit dances in harmony, whispering its artistic significance:


8, an elegant curve of eternity, embodies the balance between form and function, where beauty and practicality converge. It symbolizes the harmonious blending of elements that breathe life into extraordinary spaces.


9, a graceful loop of inspiration, encapsulates the boundless creativity that flows through our veins. It represents our ability to think beyond conventions, infusing every project with visionary design and artistic flair.


7, a poised stroke of introspection, channels the wisdom and depth we bring to each endeavour. It signifies our profound understanding of space, the art of introspection, and the transformative power of a thoughtful design process.


The last 8 symbolises our core values that revolve around our profession.



  1. Transparency: We believe in open and honest communication with our clients, partners, and team members. We strive to provide complete transparency throughout every step of the design process, ensuring trust and clarity in all our interactions.
  2. Accountability: We take full responsibility for our actions and decisions. We hold ourselves accountable for delivering exceptional results and meeting our clients' expectations. Integrity is at the core of our work, and we are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism.
  3. Ethical Practices: We adhere to a strict code of ethics, conducting our business with integrity and honesty. We maintain confidentiality, respect privacy, and operate in full compliance with legal and industry standards, ensuring our clients' interests are always protected.
  4. Authenticity: We value authenticity in our work, believing that true beauty emerges from the genuine expression of individuality. We strive to create designs that reflect the unique personalities, values, and aspirations of our clients, ensuring that each space is a true reflection of their identity.
  5. Client-Centric Approach: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen attentively to their needs, preferences, and dreams, and we prioritize their satisfaction above all else. We believe in building strong, long-lasting relationships with our clients, based on trust, respect, and mutual understanding.
  6. Quality Craftsmanship: We are committed to delivering excellence in every detail of our work. From material selection to construction techniques, we maintain uncompromising standards of quality and craftsmanship. We collaborate with skilled contractors and suppliers who share our commitment to delivering the highest level of quality.
  7. Professionalism: We conduct ourselves with the utmost professionalism, treating everyone with respect, fairness, and dignity. Our team members are knowledgeable, well-trained, and dedicated to providing exceptional service. We foster a positive and inclusive work environment, where collaboration and mutual support thrive.
  8. Continuous Improvement: We embrace a culture of learning and growth, constantly seeking new knowledge, techniques, and inspirations. We stay abreast of industry trends and innovations, ensuring that our designs are always fresh, contemporary, and relevant. We are dedicated to evolving and improving our skills to exceed the expectations of our clients.

8978 is more than just interior designers; we are storytellers, weaving narratives of beauty, functionality, and emotion into every space we touch.

Our mission is to bring dreams to life and to create spaces that resonate with the souls of those who inhabit them. We believe in the power of design to elevate lives, inspire creativity, and foster a sense of belonging.
